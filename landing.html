How to Play
Simple rules.
Huge moments.
01
Create or join a game
One player creates a game and gets a 6-digit code. Everyone else enters the code to join — no app download needed, just a browser.
02
The cup holder predicts
Whoever holds the cup predicts what will happen on the next drive — Touchdown, Field Goal, Punt, Turnover, Safety, or Missed FG.
03
Host enters the result
One person is the host and watches the game — college or pro. After each drive ends, they enter the real outcome to resolve the round.
04
Right guess = win the cup
Nail the prediction and you pocket all the points in the cup. The cup resets to 10 and moves to the next player.
05
Wrong guess = cup grows
Miss it and the cup passes to the next player with 10 more points added. The tension builds every drive.

Questions
Good to know.
Do I need to download an app?
Nope. Pass the Cup runs entirely in your browser. Open the link, enter your name, and you're in.
How many players can join?
There's no hard limit for the MVP. Works best with 2–8 players gathered around a game.
Who is the host?
The person who creates the game is the host. They watch the TV and enter drive results — they also play and can hold the cup.
What's the difference between Fumble and Interception?
We split turnovers into two separate picks so you can be more specific. Fumble = offense loses the ball on the ground. Interception = QB's pass is caught by the defense. Turnover on Downs is when the offense fails to convert on 4th down.
When does the game end?
The host decides when to end the game. Hit "End Game" after any drive to see the final scores.
Is real money involved?
Not yet — it's points only for now. Real-money wagering is coming in a future version, pending applicable regulations.
